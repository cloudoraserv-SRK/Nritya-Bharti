About Nritya Bharti

A recognized cultural education institution dedicated to preserving, promoting, and certifying Indian performing arts.

Nritya Bharti Sangeet Kala Kendra works with students, teachers, and cultural institutions to nurture excellence in dance, music, fine arts, and traditional knowledge through structured learning, examinations, and cultural platforms.

Our Mission & Vision Connect With Us
Cultural Education Music · Dance · Fine Arts Certification & Examinations

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve, promote, and strengthen Indian cultural heritage by providing structured education in music, dance, and fine arts.

We aim to create disciplined learners through systematic training, transparent examinations, and nationally recognized certification programs.

Our Vision

To become a globally respected cultural institution that bridges traditional Indian art forms with modern educational systems.

We envision a future where every learner has access to authentic cultural education regardless of age, location, or background.

Our Objectives

  • Promote Indian classical, folk, and contemporary arts
  • Offer standardized syllabus & examination systems
  • Provide certificates, diplomas & academic recognition
  • Support teachers, institutions & franchise centers
  • Create cultural platforms through events & competitions

Our History & Background

Nritya Bharti Sangeet Kala Kendra was established with the vision of safeguarding India’s vast cultural heritage by transforming traditional learning into a structured academic system.

What began as a cultural initiative has evolved into a recognized institution offering examinations, certifications, and diploma-level programs for students, teachers, and affiliated academies across India.

Over the years, the institution has actively contributed to the cultural ecosystem by organizing workshops, seminars, competitions, and large-scale cultural events at national and international levels.

Our growth reflects our commitment to discipline, authenticity, artistic excellence, and academic integrity.
