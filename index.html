Recognized & Honoured Cultural Institution

About the Organization

Nritya Bharti Sangeet Kala Kendra is a dedicated cultural education institution working towards the preservation, promotion, and structured learning of Indian classical, folk, and contemporary art forms.

Our institution provides systematic training, academic guidance, examinations, and certification programs for students, teachers, and affiliated cultural centers across the country.

  • Structured syllabus aligned with cultural learning standards
  • Certificate & diploma-level examination system
  • Qualified instructors, mentors, and examiners
  • National & international cultural programs and events

Our Vision

To create a strong foundation for cultural education by making quality training and certification accessible to learners of all age groups, while preserving the authenticity of Indian arts.

Our Mission

To nurture talent through disciplined training, transparent evaluation, and performance-based learning, encouraging students to grow academically, artistically, and professionally.

Scope of Activities

  • Dance, music, and fine arts education programs
  • Examinations, assessments & certification
  • Workshops, seminars & teacher training
  • Cultural events, competitions & stage productions

Franchise & Institutional Affiliation

Partner with Nritya Bharti Sangeet Kala Kendra as an Authorized Franchise Center and offer recognized certifications, examinations, and cultural opportunities at national and international levels.

Why Partner With Us?

Our franchise model empowers educators, academies, and cultural institutions to operate as officially recognized centers while delivering structured cultural education with credibility and growth.

  • Authority to issue participation & certification certificates
  • Access to national & international examinations
  • Recognized affiliation under a cultural institution
  • Structured syllabus, evaluation & academic support
  • Growth through events, competitions & workshops

Who Can Apply?

Dance schools, music academies, fine arts institutions, individual teachers, NGOs, and cultural organizations committed to quality education.
